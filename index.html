ALPHA-BETA TUBULIN HETERODIMER

Highly conserved heterodimers of globular α−tubulin and β−tubulin are structural elements of microtubules (MT). The αβ−dimer is widely investigated for anti-cancer drug target and architectural aspects facilitating quantum information processing. Current project aims to investigate the protein architecture, structure-activity relationship, and key features of aminoacids promoting quantum tunneling.
